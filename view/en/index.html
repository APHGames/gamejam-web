GameJam

FIT

FR 15.4 11 AM - SU 17.4. 4 PM
FIT CTU, Thákurova 9 Prague
Register

Sign up either as a team or an individual and engage in an 48-hour event to make up a game in any engine of your choosing. The event is primarily intended for students, yet anyone can apply. You will have the opportunity to benefit from regular feedback provided by a few lecturers from FIT CTU, as well as some people from the game industry during the evaluation part, such as Warhorse Studios.

The event will take place in FIT CTU (Thákurova 9 Prague, the introduction will be held in a room TH:A-1455). You can look forward to a rich program that will ease your time spent on hacking - lightning talks, a lot of food, and gaming. All pieces of information can be found on our Discord.
